Intern / Instrumentation Branch @ NASA Armstrong Flight Research Center

June 2015 - August 2015

Worked with SOFIA instrumentation team to design mechanical fixtures for sensor evaluation and testing. Wrote testing procedures and oversaw functional test and verification process. Designed and oversaw testing for thermal sensors to be evaluated for use in SOFIA instrumentation. Using AutoCAD, I designed a test fixture to verify the thermal performance of Off the Shelf thermal shutoff sensors. Using plastic bushings, the fixture was optimized to produce minimal change in thermal performance of the sensors being tested. Coordinated with machine shop to procure materials for, manufacture, and assemble the test fixture. Developed detailed test plans to test the sensors under many thermal conditions and atmospheric pressures to verify sensor performance.
