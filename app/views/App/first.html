FIRST Robots

Inspiring the next generation to build cool stuff



I've been involved in the FIRST Robotics competition since 2007. I was a student on Team 399: Eagle Robotics and FTC Team 72: Garagebots from the fall of 2009 until my high school graduation in 2013. Shortly after, I was involved in coaching several other teams in the Antelope Valley Area until 2015 when I settled in with Team 5012 Gryffingear. In 2019, when I left my last job, I also departed from Gryffingear. Since then, I've joined team 399 once again as a mentor.

I really enjoy spending my time teaching students about mechanical design, software development, project management, and life skills. The robots listed on this page are merely tools for the main goal of inspiring students to find their own personal growth and passions. I think we've made some cool robots along the way.
